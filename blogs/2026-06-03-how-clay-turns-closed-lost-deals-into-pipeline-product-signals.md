Clay's GTM team developed a system that extracts intelligence from closed lost deals stored in CRM data and call transcripts to create value across multiple teams. Using Claygent to analyze Gong recordings, the workflow identifies true loss reasons, competitive threats, and buying committee details, then routes this information to sales teams for automated re-engagement plays and to product teams for roadmap prioritization. The same continuously-running process keeps Salesforce accurate and triggers outreach automatically when market conditions shift, eliminating manual research while surfacing actionable insights from existing data.
