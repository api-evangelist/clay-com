Clay MCP integrates Clay's prospecting and enrichment capabilities directly into OpenAI's Codex, allowing sales representatives to access data and workflows without switching tools. This integration enables ops teams to maintain governance and control while giving reps the ability to research accounts, draft personalized messaging, and trigger outreach all within a single AI interface. As changes to Clay's central GTM infrastructure are made, all reps using Codex automatically benefit from those improvements.
